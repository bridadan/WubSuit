Protocol
========
TX: | 0x00 | 0x(xdata) | 0x(ydata) | 0x(zdata) | 0x(flex) | 0x(height) | 
RX: every byte sent to LED controller 


DIO0: Note capture button 
DIO1: Left foot piezo trigger 
DIO2: Right foot piezo trigger 
DIO3: BTN 1 
DIO4: BTN 2 
DIO5: BTN 3 
DIO6: BTN 4 
DIO7:
